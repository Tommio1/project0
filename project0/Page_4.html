My thoughts...


These are all 18 carat gold watches. Because of this their use is limited. Gold scratches very easily. Also only the Glashutte Original and the JLC are sufficiently water resistant for use in the shower or pool. You really shouldn't get a leather strap wet anyway as they tend to fall apart.

Investing in watches?

Some people consider watches a profitable investment. This is rarely the case. You might get lucky though. This Rolex Daytona Cosmograph that Paul Newman used to wear everyday sold at auction for $17,752,500.
